Housing Adventure!

28 April 2016

Over the past few weeks since hearing I will be living in San Francisco for my Summer internship at Lucasfilm Studios, a group of interns and I have been rigorously been searching for housing in the nations most expensive cost of living city. While it has not been easy, we have finally settled on a place to stay for the summer. We will be living just south of the city in a 5 bedroom 1 bath house. This will make life difficult in the morning but one of the more affordable options we had for all of us interns.

While the commute to work will be about 35 minutes to 45 minutes, it will be doable in a car pool between all of the interns. While we still sit out with just over a month to go until the internship starts, the thought of being on the west coast is still very exciting. Taking care of the housing has crossed off on more thing to do to prepare for the cross country trip.

Alec Carruth
