Heading to California

30 March 2016

It has Happened

After years of applying to internships and getting denied or most of the time ignored, the internship of my dreams fell into my lap and I was able to get the job. Starting this summer I will be interning for Lucasfilm in San Francisco, CA as a I.T. Operations Support Intern. The offer came as a huge shock to me and I could not believe that I was selected to work in such an amazing company doing what I love.

Living out in California will be an amazing experience and cannot wait for. It will help me grow in my field and as a person. This really is the chance of a lifetime.

Alec Carruth
